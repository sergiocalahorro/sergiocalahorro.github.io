About me

My name is Sergio, I am 25 years old and I was born in Jaén, a small city in the south of Spain. I would describe myself as a curious, hard-working, tenacious, ambitious person and a perfectionist, always willing to learn new things that can help me improve in every aspect of my life.

Since I was a child I have been passionate about video games, I enjoyed both playing them and thinking about the way they worked. For this reason, I had decided to direct my career towards working in the video game industry. It would definitely be a dream come true.
